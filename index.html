ABOUT DIEGO

Diego began his career in Boyle Heights as an organizer. In 2010 his passion for public service took him to Washington where he became the first dog to represent CA’s 34 congressional district. He is a known coalition builder who will work hard for all Americans. As a fierce advocate for reproductive rights, healthcare, and criminal justice reform, Diego is the progressive candidate you can count on.
